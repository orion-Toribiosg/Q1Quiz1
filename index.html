THE GRAVE OF THE FIREFLIES



    "The Grave of the Fireflies" is a wonderful story (both animated and live) of Setsuko and Seita, two Japanese civilians who suffer through the effects of war and loss. Their story represents the countless lives lost because of World War II.

    As these two siblings venture out for survival and struggle to live without their parents, their journey continues to break many hearts and enlighten thousands of minds on the cruel aftermath of losing a war.


    MEET SETSUKO AND SEITA


    SETSUKO

    Setsuko is the younger sister of Seita. Bubbly, imaginative, and lively -- she is the reason that Seita kept going, and the anchor that keeps him afloat 'till the very end.


    SEITA

    Seita is the older brother of Setsuko, and the protagonist of the animated film. We journey alongside him as he goes through the realities of adulthood at a young age, ensuring that his loved sister survives.
